About Me

Hello, my name is Camilo Sanchez. I'm Colombian and I study Applied Computer Science at Dalhousie University.

I'm a quick learner. I excel at picking up new skills and I really enjoy the learning process. I enjoy working in teams. I follow instructions thoroughly and if the team is in need of direction, I'm able to take a leadership role. Ever since I was a kid I've been interested in video games and design. I would love to help expand the video game industry in my home country. I want the people of Colombia to recognize the value of video games as an artistic medium.
