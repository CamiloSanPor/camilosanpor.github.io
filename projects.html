Weometry Gars

Weometry Gars is a top down shooter where you, the player, take turns with the enemies firing and dodging shots.

https://ploopjuice.itch.io/weometry-gars

❮ ❯

Controls

Keyboard and Mouse

  • WASD Keys to move
  • Mouse to aim
  • Left Mouse Click or Space to fire

Gamepad

  • Left Stick to Move
  • Right Stick to Aim
  • Right Should Button (R1 on most controllers) to shoot.
